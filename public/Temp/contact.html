Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell my artwork?
To sell your artwork, create an artist account and submit your portfolio for review. Once approved, you can list your artworks with descriptions, pricing, and licensing options.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How are shipping costs calculated?
Shipping costs are calculated based on artwork size, weight, and destination. You can view exact shipping costs before finalizing your purchase.
